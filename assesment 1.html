CHICKEN STIR-FRY
reference more variations:
  • classic chicken stir-fry(all recipe) chicken & broccoli stir-fry(food network)

A chicken stir-fry is our go-to weeknight dinner when we just do not have the energy to spend a long time in the kitchen.

This recipe is no exception.Make it as is,then use it as a jumping-off point to create your perfect chicken stir-fry.

This simple recipe is easy and yummy when the ingredients are well chosen.

ingredients

  • 2 tbs chicken breast
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper,sliced
  • 2 carrots,julienned
  • sauce1/2 cup soy sauce,1 tbsp honey,1 tbs grated ginger,2 cloves,minced galic.
preparation
  1. Saute chicken:heat oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.add chicken and cook through.remove and set aside
  2. Cook vegetables:add a bit more oil if needed.toss in broccoli,carrots,and peppers.stir-fry for 3-5 minutes until browned and cooked through.remove and set aside
  3. Combine:add the chicken back into the pan
  4. Sauce:pour the whisked sauce ingredients over the mixture.toss for 1-2 minutes untill the sauce thickens and coast everything evenly
  5. Serve:serve immediately over steamed rice or noodle
